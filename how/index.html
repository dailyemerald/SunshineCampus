How SunshineCampus works

Thank you for supporting independent student journalism in the public interest. Before you make a donation, you may to review our fundraising policies.

Editorial independence

Our newsroom will have complete control over all editorial decisions, and donors should not expect to have any role in the selection of stories or coverage areas. We will be especially cautious with potential funders whose primary purpose is to further a specific political outcome, public policy, or public opinion. Our newsroom reserves the right to decline a contribution for any reason.

Donor transparency

We will publicly disclose the name of each funder but not the amount of each donation. We will not accept anonymous donations.

Tracking your impact

Every project we produce will mention the fund and we’ll share the complete file of public records once the story is published. We’ll keep a log of all the stories on this site.

What donors receive

Each donor will receive a thank you note and letter to acknowledge our receipt of your support. This policy was adapted from policies at the Wisconsin Center for Investigative Journalism.
